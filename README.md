# 🎮 Cuphead-Inspired Game

Welcome to **Cuphead-Inspired Game**, a fast-paced, run-and-gun platformer inspired by the classic **Cuphead**! Experience challenging bosses, stylish art, and precision-based gameplay as you battle your way through whimsical yet perilous levels.  

This project is built to showcase game mechanics, enemy AI, boss fights, and parry-based resource systems, bringing the magic of 1930s cartoon-style visuals to modern gameplay.

---

## 🕹 Features

- **Challenging Boss Fights**  
  Face a variety of bosses with unique attack patterns and phases.  

- **Run-and-Gun Levels**  
  Navigate treacherous landscapes filled with hazards, enemies, and collectible items.  

- **EX Moves & Super Arts**  
  Execute special attacks by building up a card meter through damaging enemies or parrying projectiles.  

- **Parry Mechanics**  
  Time your parries on pink projectiles to gain cards and score bonuses.  

- **Dynamic Enemy Spawning**  
  Enemies appear based on level progression and player position, creating an unpredictable and exciting gameplay experience.  

- **Customizable Weapons & Abilities**  
  Switch between different weapons, each with unique damage, range, and EX attacks.  

- **Art & Animation Style**  
  Hand-drawn 2D sprites and animations inspired by vintage cartoons.  

---

## 🎯 Gameplay Mechanics

### Card System
- **Super Cards:** Use to perform EX moves or powerful Super Arts.  
- **Gaining Cards:** Hit enemies or parry pink projectiles to fill the card meter.  
- **EX Moves:** Spend 1 card to perform a weapon-specific special attack.  
- **Super Arts:** Spend 5 cards for a massive attack capable of dealing significant damage.  

### Enemy & Boss Behavior
- **Dynamic Spawning:** Enemies spawn according to area and progression rather than fixed timers.  
- **Max Active Enemies:** Each enemy type has a maximum number allowed on screen to maintain balance.  
- **Boss HP & Patterns:** Bosses have defined hit points, multiple phases, and unique attack behaviors.
